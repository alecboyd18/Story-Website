The 3 Big Pigs

Once Upon a time,

There were 3 big pigs. And the 3 Very large Pigs are hungry for wolves
The Pigs find and schase the wolf all throughtout the forest.
then the pigs came across a clearing with 3 houses that the wold could be hiding in, a straw house, a wood house or a brick house.
The 3 big pigs eat the straw house and trample the wood house and the wolf is not in either.
The pigs cannot get into the brick house, so they climb onto the roof to go through the chimney. But since they are so big, they fall through the roof onto the hot stove, and now the wolf has lots of free ham and bacon.
